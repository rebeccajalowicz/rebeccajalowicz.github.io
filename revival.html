ABOUT RESUME

Revival

Revival is a concept of a game developed in a 24 hour hackathon taking place at The College of New Jersey. It is an open world game in which the user wakes up in a house and needs to complete tasks in order to make a mask and go outside into a polluted world. From there on is it an open world game where the player needs to work to clean up the world from the pollution which is poisoning it.
