Adobe Xd Creative Challenge

Adobe hosts many 9 day creative challenges where each day is a new project. The challenge I completed was for Adobe Xd, aimed to help people build up their prototyping skills. I did the challenge from December 1st through Decmber 11th.

Day 1 - Landing Page

This piece is a simple landing page for the flower shop Jolly Florals. The goal for the look and feel for this piece is modern and elegant.

Skills Learned:

  • Making a component to be used again - Made the green explore button a component and used it again with the button labeled "Buy"
  • Background Blur - Used in the Footer

Day 2 - Interactive Map

Here I made a dark mode version of a store or restaurant locator app, similar to yelp.

Skills Learned:

  • Scroll Groups - A scroll group was used to make the map itself movable along with being able to scroll through the different restaurants on the bottom of the screen
  • Repeat Grids - Used in bottom of the screen to make multiple restaurants

Day 3 - Dropdown Menu

This piece is a notification center for people working in an office. With this notification center, users would be able to see who in their company is working at that moment and what specifically they are working on.

Skills Learned:

  • Hover States - The default state is an empty screen and hover state is the notification center sliding into the screen.
  • Auto Animate vs. Transition - Transition is used to make the hover effect over the coworkers because auto animate took longer to display the effect.

Day 4 - Card Flip

Here is a simple card flip where if you click on the card you can see someone's social media stats.

Skills Learned:

  • Auto Animate & Snap - Snap was the effect used to make this particular animation

Day 5 - Augmented Reality

This piece is a concept for what an augrmented reality experience for a workplace could be. The person using this is someone going to an interview, the AR shows him exaclty where he needs to go, along with who else is in the office.

Skills Learned:

  • 3d Transforms - Used to make the triangles at the bottom appear to be leading away from the screen along with the worker id's to be angled in front of their desks.

Day 6 - Presentation Template

Here is just a template for a possible presentation. Instead of using something like google slides or powerpoint, I would potentially be able to use Xd as well.

Skills Learned:

  • Trigger using keys and gamepad - Used right arrow key to transition between slides in protyping mode.

Day 7 - Slide Out Menu

This is just a sample navigation bar for an app on a tablet or desktop. This piece is just showing how the navigation in particular would work.

Skills Learned:

  • This piece is more of a combination of skills previously learned, albeit, combined.

Day 8 - Browsable Library

Here is a sample landing page for a music library. I tried to make an app in dark mode again, this time it was more so because I think it worked well with the mood of the page.

Skills Learned:

  • Similarly to the slide out menu this piece is more of a combination of skills previously learned. Although I feel like with this piece I focused more on layout so I developed more of an understanding in that area.

Day 9 - Social Profile Card

Similarly to the card flip this is just a card showing social media stats

Skills Learned:

  • Stack - the whole card itself was group into a stack.
